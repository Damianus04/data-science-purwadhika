Bandai ULTRAMAN Ultra 500 Figure List


Photos courtesy of Bandai Co. © Tsuburaya Productions

Source: Bandai Co., Ltd.
Official Site: b-boys.jp/ultra_hero500 (Japan)

In conjunction with the July 2013 debut of Tsuburaya Productions’ ULTRAMAN GINGA (ウルトラマンギンガ, Urutoraman Ginga) television series, Bandai launched a new wave of collectibles under the “Ultra Hero 500” brand. Based on the Spark Dolls concept of GINGA, which had Ultramen and monsters transformed into toys by a mysterious force, the 500 line primarily focused on PVC (soft vinyl) figures with an average height of 14cm (5.5 inches). There was also a set of Gashopon (capsule) figures, larger DX monsters, and characters from the GIANT MONSTER RUSH: ULTRA FRONTIER card game and computer animated shorts.

In 2015, Bandai rebooted the toy line yet again as “Ultraman X Ultra Hero X” to tie-in with the latest Ultra show, ULTRAMAN X (ウルトラマンＸ, Urutoraman Ekkusu, 2015).

Note: English names for Ultramen and monsters taken from Bandai and Tsuburaya Pro materials.
